From Taiwan to the World

Since its establishment in 2006, Gong Cha has blossomed to over 15 countries worldwide and growing. At Gong Cha, we believe in serving quality bubble tea. Our teas can be blended with a variety of fruits, toppings and creative mixes. Gong Cha today is one of the most recognized bubble tea brands around the world.

Gong Cha prides itself on offering freshly brewed tea and toppings to our customers everyday. Known for premium tea and service, Gong Cha represents the highest standards of tea culture. Here at Gong Cha Tampa, we deliver a potpourri of drinks alongside our bubble waffles and daily specials!
