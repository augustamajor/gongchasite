Frequently Asked Questions

    General questions

    + Do you accept phone/online orders?

    We accept phone orders! Drinks will be fully completed once they are paid for in-person at the shop, and food will be made a few minutes before your arrival to ensure freshness.

    Please note that we do not offer delivery services, so orders will have to be picked up from our store.

    + How does your rewards system work?

    Every time you order, please input your phone number or email address. Once you have bought ten drinks with us, you'll get a free medium no topping drink.

    Please use the Clover app or present your QR code to the cashier to redeem.

    + What is the best way to provide feedback, ask questions, make menu requests, or address complaints?

    Please reach out to us via our email ( gongchatampa@gmail.com) or message us on Instagram (@gongchatampa). We're grateful for all customer feedback!

    Drink Questions

    + What is boba?

    Boba is made from tapioca, which is a gluten-free starch extracted from the root of a cassava plant.

    + What is the difference between black boba and white boba?

    + Does your creamer have dairy? Do you have any drinks without dairy?

    Our plant-based creamer powder and some of the other drink powders contain dairy. If you are looking for non-dairy drinks, we recommend the brewed teas or wintermelon drinks!

    + Do you have any non-dairy alternatives?

    Yes, we have soy milk and almond milk - just ask for it!

    + Does you use honey for your black tapioca pearls?

    Nope, we use brown sugar.

    + Do you guys have _ drink/topping?

    We are continuously adding new drinks and toppings to our menu; if you ask, we can try to make it if we have the ingredients on hand or consider adding it to our menu in the future!

    Food Questions

    + How can we pay for the items on the Daily Special menu?

    We accept cash only for the items on our Daily Special menu. We have cash on hand as change for larger bills.

    + What are bubble waffles? What do they taste like?

    A bubble waffle is a Chinese sweet and custardy snack made in a special waffle iron. It includes eggs, flour, milk and sugar. We also have a variety of toppings and ice cream for them.
